    A M E N I T I E S

    Outdoor Pool

    We have 1 outdoor pool on the top of the building. Join us at the pool for some breathaking views, to catch some rays, or to swim some laps! We also offer a poolside bar! Pool hours are from 8:00 am to 10:00 pm.

    On-site Bar & Restaurant

    Tiffanie's Tastes, our on-site restaurant and bar, offers a wide variety of international and American cuisine. The menu is inspired by all of Tiffanie's favorite dishes. Come join us and try our famous 6 course meal!

    Spa

    Our spa offers many services to help relax, cleanse, and rejuvenate! We offer massages, facials, manicures, pedicures, and even romantic mud-baths! Our spa hours are 9:00 am - 5:00 pm.

    Fitness Center

    Our fitness center has everything you need to get a great workout in! There are weights as well as treadmills and elipitcal machines. We offer free waters and towels. The fitness center is open 24 hours!

    E X P L O R E

    Honolulu Zoo

    Take a 5 minute walk to see some amazing creatures at the Honolulu Zoo! Guests of Tiffanie's receive $2 off admission!

    Diamond Head Mountain

    Take a guieded tour and hike up Diamond Head Mountain! We have daily excursions that leave at 7:30 am.

    Waikiki Beach

    Bed & Breakfast at Tiffanie's is located right on Waikiki Beach. We have a private beach of over 200 ft long, and offer beach towels, cabanas, and chairs & umbrellas for rent as well. Rentals are available from 8 am - 6 pm.

    Shopping

    Spend hours shopping along the streets of Honolulu.
